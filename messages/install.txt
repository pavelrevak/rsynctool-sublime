RsyncTool
=========

Thank you for installing RsyncTool!

Quick Start:
1. Ctrl+Shift+P (Cmd+Shift+P on macOS) → "RSYNC: New Project..."
2. Edit .rsyncproject - configure target and sources
3. Ctrl+Alt+R (Cmd+Option+R on macOS) to sync

Or right-click on file/folder → RsyncTool → Add to Sources/Exclude

Documentation: https://github.com/pavelrevak/rsynctool-sublime
